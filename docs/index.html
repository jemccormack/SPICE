Other SPICE Resources

There are a great many helpful SPICE tutorials, models, and examples, a selection of which are:-

Tutorials

eCircuit Center has a diverse collection of circuit simulations for beginner, intermediate and advanced levels, but not targeted a particular SPICE simulator.

Simon Bramble provides a helpful series of tutorials to get up to speed quickly using LTSpice IV/XVII©.

Models, libraries, symbols, example circuits for LTSpice.

A wealth of helpful models, including libraries for whole classes of devices (such as 74HC series - see 74HC_v.lib and symbols 74HC.zip), and much more, is available from the LTSpice users group.

The LTSpice wiki has useful information on LTSpice, including undocumented features. This site also catalogues many libraries/models found in LTSpice users group, e.g. see 74HC lib.

THD Analyser is an interesting LTSpice tool for measuring harmonic distortion. If you understand Fourier series and transforms, and you are impatient to learn more advanced SPICE techniques - study this tool. Search for THDAnalyzer.zip on LTSpice users group/files.
